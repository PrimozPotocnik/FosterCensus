• name: the name of the graph. "CAT" reffers to "cubic arc-transitive", while the numbers n and k in CAT(n,k) indicate the number of vertices and the index of the graph among all the graphs on n vertices in this census
  • bip: "b" if the graph is bipartite, "n" if it is not
  • girth: the girth (i.e. the length of a shortest cycle)
  • diam: the diameter of the graph
  • IsCay: "Y" if the graph is Cayley, "N" otherwise
  • #stab: the order of the vertex-stabiliser in the automorphism group of the graph (as proved by Tutte, possible values are 3, 6, 12, 24, and 48)
  • s_v: the largest s such that the graph is s-arc-transitive
  • type: the DjokoviÄ‡-Miller amalgam type of the automorphism group
