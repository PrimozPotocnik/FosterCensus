• name: the name of the graph. "CSS" reffers to "cubic semisymmetric", while the numbers n and k in CSS(n,k) indicate the number of vertices and the index of the graph among all the graphs on n vertices in this census
  • girth: the girth (i.e. the length of a shortest cycle) of the graph
  • diam: the diameter of the graph
  • #stab: the order of the vertex-stabiliser in the automorphism group of the graph
  • s_v, s_u: the largest values of s and t such that the graph is locally s-arc-transitive at v and locally t-arc-transitive at u for two adjacent vertices v and u
  • type: the Goldscmidt amalgam type of the automorphism group
